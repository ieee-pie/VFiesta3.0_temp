About V-Fiesta 2.0

V-Fiesta 2.0 stands as the pinnacle event, meticulously organized by IEEE PIE Kerala Section and graciously hosted by IEEE SB FISAT. As the flagship event of IEEE PIE KS, this occasion epitomizes a convergence of innovation, entrepreneurship, and insightful discourse. With a rich tapestry of interactive sessions, expert discussions, and engaging activities, V-Fiesta 2.0 aims to foster a dynamic platform for exchange and exploration. It's a celebration of visionary ideas and transformative thinking, serving as a catalyst for both aspiring entrepreneurs and established thought leaders.
During this remarkable event, attendees will have the opportunity to engage in thought-provoking discussions led by industry pioneers through expert panels and keynote speeches. Interactive workshops will offer invaluable insights, equipping attendees with practical skills and strategies essential for entrepreneurial success.

About IEEE PIE KERALA SECTION

IEEE PIE is a prestigious global platform dedicated to fostering a vibrant culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among IEEE members and the wider community. PIE serves as a catalyst, inspiring individuals to push the boundaries of their creativity and empowering them to transform their innovative ideas into successful and sustainable businesses that drive social and economic progress.

About Our Host

The host of V-Fiesta 2.0, the prestigious event organized by IEEE PIE, is FISAT (Federal Institute of Science and Technology), located in Angamaly, Ernakulam. FISAT is a renowned institution known for its commitment to excellence in education and fostering a vibrant academic environment. With state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated faculty, and a student body brimming with talent, FISAT has been selected as the ideal host for V-Fiesta 2.0. Their strong track record in organizing successful events, coupled with their enthusiasm and passion for creating memorable experiences, ensures that V-Fiesta 2.0 will be a grand success under their able leadership. FISAT's location in Angamaly, a bustling town in Ernakulam district, offers convenient access and a vibrant atmosphere for participants to immerse themselves in the event's activities. With FISAT as the host, participants can expect an exceptional event filled with innovation, learning, and networking opportunities.
